Bio:

Founder of i am a film Director / Editor Graduated from high cinema institute of Egypt 2011 Started working as assistant director in feature films like – kheyana mashroua , Hena maysara ,Dokan Shehata Also in some Tv Series like El Gamaa Assistant Director in more than 10 Tv commercials Editor on set in Tv Series like Zay el Ward , Moga Hara Assistant Editor in Feature Documentaries like The Square Edited more than 20 short films and documentaries like 130 km to heaven , The red heel , Hadra , Land of strangers , to the past , The journey of searching for abo el araby Made my first short movie as a director called " six " 2013 that the script had won a fund competition in the Egyptian film center – Egyptian Ministry of Culture in 2012 that won 5 international prizes and had 25 official selections Best screen play ( Tangier international film festival ) Morocco Best Film ( Art Deco Film Festival ) Brazil Grand Prix ( Casablanca Short Film Festival ) Morocco Naoussa Prize ( Naoussa International Filml Festival ) Greece Special Mention ( Alexandria Mediterranean film festival ) Egypt Was selected among 40 african filmmakers to attended Durban Talents ( Berlinale talents in durban film festival ) in 2014 Worked as coordinator in 7th tangier international film festival in 2014's edition Work as the general coordinator in cinema of tomorrow international competition “ Cairo international film festival short film competition ( from the 1st edition of the competition in 2014 till 3rd edition in 2016) Was selected among 20 arab filmmakers to attend Beirut Talents ( Berlinale talents in Beirut for arab film makers ) in 2016-12- 21 Made my second short film My Window 2016 that is still running in film festivals Was screened in 20 film festivals and awarded Best Film in the 10th Tangier international film festival , the laurel of achievement in Cinema Grand Prix 2016 Indonesia Founder and managing director of www.iamafilm.com.
